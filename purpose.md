# Industrial Standards Wiki Purpose

This wiki is designed to manage industrial standards knowledge with focus on:

## Core Goals

1. **Standardization Management**: Track national (GB), industry (JB), provincial (DB), and enterprise (QB) standards
2. **Equipment Knowledge**: Maintain technical specifications for industrial equipment
3. **Parameter Tracking**: Record technical parameters and their requirements across standards
4. **Quality Assurance**: Ensure complete and accurate information extraction from source documents

## Knowledge Dimensions

- **Standards**: Complete standard documents with scope, requirements, and test methods
- **Equipment**: Technical specifications, applications, and related standards
- **Parameters**: Definitions, units, requirements by standard, and test methods
- **Inspection Items**: Test procedures, acceptance criteria, and inspection rules

## Quality Principles

1. **Structure Retention**: Preserve document structure (25% weight)
2. **Content Retention**: Capture all technical content (30% weight)
3. **Parameter Completeness**: Extract all technical parameters (20% weight)
4. **Reference Completeness**: Track all standard references (15% weight)
5. **Inspection Completeness**: Include all inspection items (10% weight)

## Relation Types

- **Replaces**: Standard replacement relationships (full/partial)
- **Conflicts**: Technical contradictions between standards
- **References**: Cross-references between standards
- **Scope Overlap**: Overlapping application scopes
